Laughing Through the Chaos

In this uproarious comedy set in the bustling streets of New York City, follow the misadventures of three unlikely roommates who find themselves caught in a whirlwind of chaos. When uptight accountant Mark, eccentric artist Lily, and free-spirited musician Mike are forced to share an apartment due to skyrocketing rent prices, hilarity ensues as their contrasting personalities clash in the most unexpected ways. From disastrous dinner parties to zany attempts at DIY home repairs, "Laughing Through the Chaos" is a riotous romp that proves laughter truly is the best medicine.

Rating: 2/5

Age Limit: PG-16

Hilarity at High School

.

Step back in time to the 1980s with this side-splitting comedy that takes place in the halls of Ridgemont High School. When slacker student Dave and his motley crew of friends hatch a wild scheme to throw the ultimate end-of-year party, they unwittingly set off a chain of hilarious events that culminate in chaos and mayhem. From wacky disguises and madcap pranks to unexpected romantic entanglements, "Hilarity at High School" is a nostalgic rollercoaster ride that will leave you laughing out loud..

Rating: 1/5

Age Limit: ALL

The Great Vacation Caper

Join the wacky antics of the Johnson family as they embark on a cross-country road trip of epic proportions in this side-splitting comedy extravaganza. When workaholic dad Frank decides it's time for some quality family bonding, he packs up his reluctant wife, adventurous daughter, and mischievous son for the ultimate vacation adventure. From quirky roadside attractions and bizarre encounters with eccentric locals to a series of comically calamitous mishaps, "The Great Vacation Caper" is a laugh-out-loud journey filled with unforgettable moments and uproarious laughter..

Rating: 4.5/5
