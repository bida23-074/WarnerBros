RAMPAGE

Rampage" is a thrilling action-packed film that revolves around the unexpected consequences of a genetic experiment gone wrong. In a desperate attempt to manipulate DNA, a rogue geneticist unwittingly unleashes chaos upon the world when a group of animals becomes infected with a dangerous pathogen. As a result, a gentle albino gorilla named George, along with other creatures, undergoes a radical transformation, turning into gigantic and ferocious beasts.

Amidst the ensuing pandemonium, primatologist Davis Okoye, played by Dwayne Johnson, forms an unlikely alliance with a genetic engineer named Dr. Kate Caldwell, portrayed by Naomie Harris.

Rating: 4.5/5

Age Limit: PG-13

HURT LOCKER

"Aquarium" is a captivating drama that delves into the intricacies of human relationships against the backdrop of a mesmerizing underwater world. Set in a coastal town renowned for its vibrant marine life, the film follows the intertwined lives of several individuals whose paths intersect in unexpected ways. At the heart of the story is Sarah, a marine biologist deeply connected to the ocean and its inhabitants. Through her eyes, viewers are transported into the serene yet enigmatic realm of the aquarium, where she works tirelessly to unravel the mysteries of the sea.

Through its rich tapestry of characters and evocative storytelling, the film invites audiences to dive deep into the depths of the human spirit and emerge with a newfound appreciation.

Rating: 2.5/5

Age Limit: PG-16

FUEGO SALVAJE

In the heart of bustling Mexico City, amidst the chaos of urban life and the echoes of political unrest, "Fuego Salvaje" blazes onto the screen as a fiery testament to the indomitable spirit of the Mexican people. Set against the backdrop of the tumultuous 1980s, this adrenaline-fueled action extravaganza follows the journey of Juan Ramirez, a rugged ex-Marine turned vigilante, as he takes on the criminal underworld with unmatched ferocity.

With its unforgettable characters, jaw-dropping stunts, and electrifying soundtrack, "Fuego Salvaje" blazes a trail through the annals of Mexican cinema, leaving an indelible mark on audiences and cementing its status as a timeless classic of the action genre.

Rating: 5/5

Age Limit: LV-18
